Why Face Lighting Matters in Color Analysis

Lighting has a major impact on color detection, whether you're using our digital computer vision system or assessing your features with the naked eye. Light contains color temperatures that change how colors appear.

How Bad Lighting Distorts Skin Metrics

  • Distorts Surface Skin Tone: Yellow lights falsely shift cool skin profiles into a warm profile.
  • Changes Undertone Readings: Shadows cast blue tones across cheeks, creating inaccurate cool results.
  • Reduces Analysis Accuracy: Low light prevents software from properly tracking contrast levels.

For the most accurate results, use natural daylight, avoid heavy filters, face the camera directly, and ensure your face is clearly visible. Good lighting allows AI systems to provide highly reliable, personalized recommendations.
