Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how AI Color Analysis ("we", "us", or "our"), accessible at https://aicoloranalysis.online, handles your information when you use our website. We have written this in plain English because we believe you deserve to understand exactly what happens to your data — without needing a law degree to read it.

The short version: we do not collect, store, or share your photos or personal biometric data. The tool runs entirely in your browser and your images never leave your device.

🔒 Your photo stays on your device. When you upload a photo for color analysis, it is processed locally in your browser using client-side JavaScript execution loops. It is never sent to our servers. We never see it, never store it, and never have access to it.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

To be completely clear about what we do not do:

  • We do not collect, upload, or store your photos or selfies anywhere on external hardware networks.
  • We do not create persistent user accounts or store profile tables.
  • We do not ask for your name, payment methods, or personal background information to use the tool.
  • We do not track which results you received or what your calculated color season was.
  • We do not sell your personal data to data brokers or third parties.

2. How the Color Analysis Tool Works

When you upload a photo, your browser reads the image file directly on your device and runs the color analysis entirely within that active browser session window. The pixel data from your photo is used only to calculate your skin undertone and generate your color recommendations. Once you close or refresh the page, that data is gone — it is not saved anywhere, not even temporarily on our end.

This means there is no risk of your photos being accessed, leaked, or misused by us, because we simply never receive them in the first place.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Like most websites, we use cookies and web identifiers to help the site function properly. Cookies are small text files stored in your browser. We use them for three purposes:

  • Site preferences — Remembering whether you have custom app settings or Dark Mode interface themes turned on.
  • Performance Monitoring — We utilize Google Analytics to monitor general web traffic trends, identify broken links, and measure page load efficiency across different global regions. This analytics engine captures non-personally identifiable metrics such as anonymized IP locations, browser types, and session times.
  • Advertising — We display ads through Google AdSense to keep this service free for everyone. Google uses cookies to show you relevant advertisements based on your browsing actions across the web.

4. Google AdSense and Third-Party Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on this website. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads based on your previous visits to this and other websites. These cookies allow Google to show you ads that may be relevant to your interests.

You can easily opt out of personalized advertising routes by visiting Google's Ad Settings page. You can also opt out through the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page.

Google's use of advertising cookies is governed completely by Google's own Privacy Policy.

5. Your Rights Under GDPR (European Users)

If you are located in the European Union or European Economic Area, you have certain rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). These include the right to access, correct, or delete any personal data we hold about you.

In practice, since we do not collect or store any personal data about you through the use of our tool, there is very little for us to provide, correct, or delete. The only data that may be associated with your visit is the standard server log data that most web hosts automatically generate (such as your IP address and the pages you visited), which is handled automatically and not used to identify you personally.

If you have a question about your data rights, you can contact us at aicoloranalysis.online@gmail.com and we will respond within 48 hours.

6. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has used our site, please be reassured that the tool does not store any images or personal data. No information from any user is retained after the browser session ends.

If you have any concerns, please contact us at aicoloranalysis.online@gmail.com.

7. External Links

Our blog posts and pages may occasionally link to external websites for reference. Once you leave our site, this Privacy Policy no longer applies. We recommend checking the privacy policy of any external site you visit, as we have no control over how those sites handle your data.
