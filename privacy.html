At AI Color Analysis, accessible from https://aicoloranalysis.online, your privacy is our highest priority. This policy explains what information we process and how it is protected.

1. Human Skin Metric Pixel Processing

Our application uses client-side web browser processing to analyze your seasonal profile. When you upload an image file or stream your video camera feed, all structural tracking actions happen entirely on your computer or phone. We do not transmit, collect, upload, or save your images or face data to any server location.

2. Google AdSense Advertising Cookies

We partner with third-party networks like Google AdSense to serve advertisements on our web platform. Google uses tracking cookies (such as the DART cookie profile) to serve dynamic advertisements based on your browsing actions across our platform and other digital channels. You can easily manage or disable personalized cookies by visiting the Google Ad Settings dashboard.

3. Age Restrictions & Children's Data

Our platform services do not target or knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13. If you believe a minor has interacted with our local processing cache frameworks, no photo data is stored, and closing the browser tab wipes out all localized memory structures instantly.
