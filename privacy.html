Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

At AI Color Analysis, we take your personal privacy incredibly seriously. This policy outlines exactly how image media data and analytics information are processed across our platform application layer.

1. Local Client-Side Face Processing

Our platform runs completely on client-side technology stack frameworks. When you turn on your camera stream or upload a selfie file, the image evaluation takes place inside your browser cache. Your photos, face metrics, and personal pixel matrices are never uploaded, sent, or saved to any remote web server.

2. Temporary Device RAM Allocation

Any data captured by the application is held temporarily in your hardware device's running memory loop. The exact moment you close your web browser tab or refresh the home domain url, all processing tracks are discarded instantly and cannot be recovered.

3. Third-Party Advertising (Google AdSense)

We use Google AdSense to serve ads on our website. Google may use tracking cookies to show ads based on your visits to this and other sites. You can choose to opt-out of personalized advertising tracks by adjusting your Google Ad Settings dashboard directly.

4. Web Performance Cookies

We use standard web browser LocalStorage mechanics to remember your user dashboard preference choices—specifically whether you have active light settings or Dark Mode interface toggles applied.

5. Contacting Us

If you have any questions regarding these tracking frameworks or modern security practices, feel free to use our official Contact Page channel layout.
