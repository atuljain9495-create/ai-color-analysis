        Frequently Asked Questions

        Everything you need to know about our AI Color Analysis technology.

        How accurate is the AI Color Analysis?

        Our app achieves high accuracy by using client-side computer vision models to track your facial structure and analyze active pixel balances across your cheeks and forehead. To get the most accurate result, make sure you are in natural, indirect daylight and aren't wearing makeup or tinted glasses.

        Are my photos or selfies saved on a server?

        No, absolutely not. All image extraction, face mapping, and color sampling happens directly inside your web browser's temporary memory (client-side processing). Your photos are never uploaded to our servers, and all cached data is permanently erased the instant you reload or close the page.

        What is the difference between skin tone and undertone?

        Your surface skin tone is the color you see on the surface (like fair, medium, tan, or deep), which can change with sun exposure or seasons. Your undertone is the subtle, permanent biological color signature underneath your skin (Warm, Cool, or Neutral) that never changes.

        Why does the analyzer say "Unable to isolate clear skin pixels"?

        This message appears if your lighting is too dark, washed out, or if there are deep shadows falling across your face. It can also happen if your face is positioned too far away from the camera. Move closer to a clear window or brighten your room's lighting and try again!

        How should I use my seasonal color results?

        You can use your personalized seasonal profile (like Deep Winter or Warm Spring) to easily pick out clothing colors, hair dyes, makeup shades, and metallic jewelry choices that naturally complement your face, helping you look your best effortlessly.
