        Frequently Asked Questions

        Everything you need to know about our AI Color Analysis technology.

        How accurate is the AI Color Analysis?

        Our app achieves high accuracy by using client-side computer vision to track your facial structure and analyse pixel balances across your cheeks and forehead. For best results, use natural indirect daylight without makeup or tinted glasses.

        Are my photos or selfies saved on a server?

        No. All face mapping and colour sampling happens directly in your browser's memory. Your photos are never uploaded to our servers, and all data is permanently cleared when you reload or close the page.

        How does gender detection work?

        We use face-api.js, a privacy-first machine learning library that runs entirely in your browser. It estimates gender and approximate age from facial features locally on your device — nothing is sent anywhere. This helps us personalise clothing and accessory recommendations for you.

        What is the difference between skin tone and undertone?

        Your skin tone is the surface colour (fair, medium, tan, deep) which can change with sun exposure. Your undertone is the permanent biological colour beneath the skin surface (Warm, Cool, or Neutral) that never changes throughout your life.

        Why does the analyser say it cannot detect clear skin pixels?

        This usually means the lighting is too dark or the face is too far from the camera. Move closer to a window with natural light and try again without heavy filters.

        How should I use my seasonal colour results?

        Your seasonal profile (like Deep Winter or Warm Spring) tells you which clothing colours, hair tones, makeup shades, and jewellery metals naturally complement your face. Use it as a guide when shopping to avoid colours that wash you out.
