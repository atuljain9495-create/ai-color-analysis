Common Color Mistakes People Make

Many people wear specific colors simply because they are currently trendy or look beautiful on an online model, rather than considering whether they complement their personal complexion.

Four Outfitting Pitfalls to Avoid

  • Clashing with Undertones: Forcing a cool slate gray onto a warm autumn skin profile, making them look instantly fatigued.
  • Choosing Overly Bright Shades: Selecting screaming neon variations that wash out delicate, soft features.
  • Ignoring Natural Contrast Levels: Wearing monochromatic pastel colors when you have striking high-contrast dark hair and light skin.
  • Following Trends Blindly: Buying seasonal trends that fight aggressively against your personal color palette.

The most flattering wardrobe is built around colors that naturally enhance your features rather than overpowering them. Focus on what highlights *you*, not the trend cycle.
